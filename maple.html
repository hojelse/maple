F249

Binomialfordelt stokatisk variabel \(X\) med antalsparameter \(n\) og sandsynlighedsparameter \(p\) $$ X \sim b(n,p) $$

F250

Binomialkoefficient \(K(n,r)\) $$ K(n,r)= \binom{a}{b} = { n! \over r!(n-r)! } $$

F252

Sandsynlighedsfunktion for binomialfordelt stokastisk variabel \(X\) $$ P(X=r)=K(n,r) \cdot p^r(1-p)^{n-r} $$

M252

$$ X=RandomVariable(Binomial(n,p)) $$ $$ PDF(X,r) $$ $$ X=RandomVariable(Binomial(100,0.5)): $$ $$ PDF(X,45) $$
$$ 0.04847429663\ Dirac(0.) $$

F253

Middelværdi \(μ\) $$ μ = n \cdot p $$

M253

$$ μ = 100 \cdot 0.5 $$
$$ μ = 50.0 $$
$$ μ = Mean(X) $$
$$ μ = 50.0 $$

F254

Spredning \( σ \) $$ σ = \sqrt{n \cdot p \cdot (1 - p)} $$

M254

$$ σ = \sqrt{100 \cdot 0.5 \cdot (1 - 0.5)} $$
$$ σ = 5.000000000 $$
$$ σ = \sqrt{Variance(X)} $$
$$ σ = 5.000000000 $$
