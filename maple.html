F194

De partielle afledede af \( f(x,y) \) mht. \(x\) og \(y\) $$ diff(f(x,y),x) = {\partial \over \partial \ x} $$ $$ diff(f(x,y),y) = {\partial \over \partial \ y} $$

F194

\( f(x,y) := x^3 - 3xy + y^3: \)

\( {\partial \over \partial \ x} f(x,y) \)
$$ 3x^2 - 3y $$
\( {\partial \over \partial \ y} f(x,y) \)
$$ 3y^2 - 3x $$

F195

F195

$$ grad = \begin{bmatrix} 3x^2 - 3y \\ 3y^2 - 3x \end{bmatrix} $$

F197

Stationært punkt \( P_0(x_0,y_0,z_0) \) for \(f\) $$ \nabla f(x,y) = \vec 0 = \begin{bmatrix} 0 \\ 0 \end{bmatrix} $$ $$ f_x'(x_0, y_0) = 0 \ \textrm{og} \ f_y'(x_0, y_0) = 0 $$
Arten af stationære punkter for \(f\), hvor
$$ r=f_{xx}''(x,y) , $$ $$ s=f_{xy}''(x,y) = f_{yx}''(x,y) $$ $$ t=f_{yy}''(x,y) $$
\( f(x,y) := x^3 - 3xy + y^3 : \)
\( r = {\partial^2 \over \partial \ x^2} f(x,y) \)
$$ r = 6x $$
\( r = {\partial^2 \over \partial \ x \ \partial \ y} f(x,y) \)
$$ s = -3 $$
\( r = {\partial^2 \over \partial \ y^2} f(x,y) \)
$$ t = 6y $$
